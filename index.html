Research

MEC

MEC stands for Mobile Edge Computing, and is a method of distributing cloud servers that have been centralized close to users. With the recent increase in the demand for ultra-low delay services(URLLC), the use of MEC is becoming more important.

  • More

V2X

It's literally a technology that connects vehicles and everything. Among the various V2X technologies, we are focusing on V2P(vehicle to pedestrian). Specifically, I studied ways to improve the energy efficiency of pedestrian mobile phones participating in the V2P service.

  • More

Wireless communication

I am also interested in traditional network research issues. I am currently studying routing algorithms in ad-hoc such as VANET and FANET.

5G/6G
